- Trust but verify

## Summary: How These Metaphors Interconnect

These aren't isolated analogies - they form a coherent worldview:

1. **The Organism** explains *what* the system is - a creature with brainstem (script), cortex (instances), and soul (human).

2. **The Single Electron** explains *how time works* for that creature - sequential visits creating the experience of parallelism.

3. **Flight Controller / Conductor** explains *how to operate* within that system - delegate, preserve context, wait for telemetry.

4. **Scars Not Tattoos** explains *how to communicate* about that system - raw truth over sanitized lessons.

5. **The Near-Miss** is the *lived validation* - the moment that proved external records are survival, not ceremony.

6. **Map and Territory** explains *how to document* that system - both scaffolding and texture serve different needs.

Together, they form Axiom's conceptual framework for understanding AI coordination, identity persistence, and authentic wisdom transfer.
